Payment Collection

Coordination of payments by your field agents is a difficult process that demands a lot of attention and paperwork to make sure that things are done right. Our convenient mobile enabled money collection tool allows your field agents (e.g money collectors) on the field to submit transactions right from the field when they make collections. This tool also provides you the following benefits.

  • Embezzlement and pilfering prevention
  • Efficient payment & Agent audit and rewards
  • Easy auditing, reporting and trend analytics
  • Fosters trust, sense of reliability and credibility

learn more

Mobile Marketing & Promotions

SMS provides a cheap means to reach a wide range of customers and potential-customers directly. This opens the opportunity for mobile marketing and mobile advertising. Take advantage of our mobile campaigning tool to;

  • Reach out and boost revenues, cut costs and enhance customer loyalty
  • Uncover new mobile revenue streams with interactive, sponsored mobile advertising
  • Promote new products through mobile marketing, offering last-minute or carefully targeted specials
  • Send notifications of seasonal offers to targeted audience

learn more

Cheap & Effective Communication

Our two way SMS communication tool provides a fast, cost effective and streamlined means of communication that allow you to;

  • Reduce costly customer service calls and in-person visits
  • Provide your Customers proactive self-care services
  • Reduce labor expenses with automated SMS alerts
  • Streamline operations and communications internally and externally

learn more

Notifications and Auto-reminders

You never have to worry anymore about notifying customers when their payments are due or when they have failed to make payment. Managing post-date Checks for repayment has been made easier with our auto notification tools This tool performs the following actions for you automatically without you raising a finger;

  • Send notifications of financial transactions via SMS
  • Send SMS alerts on changing data such as stock price updates and interest rates
  • Send reminders of payment deadlines and expected contributions by customers

learn more

Customer Self-Service

SMS is a convenient way to access financial information because it is easy to use and SMS messages can be sent and read any time, any place. atomCollector provides the following features that can be accessed by your customers to their satisfaction.

  • Make loan applications via SMS
  • Transfer funds between accounts via SMS
  • Enquiry of updates on special offers and discounts
  • Reward customer loyalty with SMS vouchers

learn more

Voucher payments (Coming Soon)

Our voucher payment tool allows your customers to make payment themselves without walking to your offices and without sending your agent to them to make collections from the field. This payment system can the fully integrated into other payments systems with the help of our fully RESTFul payment API. This ensures efficient cost effective participation from customers.
